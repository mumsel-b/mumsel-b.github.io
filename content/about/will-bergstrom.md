title: "Will Bergstrom"
description: "Author and collaborator — grandson of Anne-Marit Bergstrom"
---

Will Bergstrom is Anne-Marit's grandson and a gifted writer who collaborated with her on two children's books — *The Magic Telescope* (2007) and *People of the Prairie* (2008), both illustrated by Anne-Marit. His storytelling and her paintings made for a natural partnership across two generations.
